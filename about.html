About Us

Objective

To enhance the efficiency in the current approval process for n approving and regulating technical education institutions in India.

  • Clarity
  • Accuracy
  • AI Support

Institutes and stakeholders would have a clear understanding of the specific requirements and guidelines outlined by AICTE, facilitating efficient compliance.

The portal would dynamically generate the required documentation templates, and fee structures based on the specific course requirements inputted by the institute.

With a user-friendly portal providing comprehensive and clear instructions, institutes are more likely to adhere accurately to AICTE's guidelines.

This would result in improved compliance and higher-quality submissions, reducing the need for reworks and ensuring smoother and faster approval processes.

The portal would significantly reduce the time and effort required for institutes to interpret and navigate the Approval Process Handbook.

It can provide AI assistance and support, offering clarification on specific queries or concerns raised by institutes during the approval process.
